About Mamamoo

Mamamoo (Korean:마마무) is a South Korean girlgroup under the entertainment company Rainbow Bridge World. The group consists of 4 members Solar, Moonbyul, Wheein and Hwasa, and had made their debut on June 18, 2014 with their single "Mr. Ambiguous". The group is primarily known for their Retro, R&B and Jazz concepts, but their discography also displays their use of other various musical genres. Not only that but they are also widely known for their powerful vocal performances, being nominated for awards like "Best Vocal Performance Female" In the Mnet Asian Music Awards for 5 years in a row and winning in 2020 as well as winning the "Vocal Artist" Award in the Genie Music Award in 2019. On top of that Mamamoo have managed to gain quite a few awards ranging from "Performer of the Year - Singer" in the Korea PD Awards in 2019 to "Top 10 Artists" in the Melon Music Awards in 2016, 2018 and 2019. Besides being known as a group, each member has their solo careers.

Solar

Kim Yong-Sun (Korean: 김용선, born February 21, 1991) or better known as Solar was born in Gangseo-ru, Seoul, South Korea and had graduated from Hanyang University with a major in tourism. She is the leader and a main vocalist of Mamamoo and is the oldest of the 4 and as each member is assigned a color, Solar's color is pink. Before the group's debut, she had participated in MBC's King of Mask Singer and had released a single of Solar Emotion, a project that she had started in 2015 and had ended in 2018 as she released it in parts. She has also had her hand in composing various songs and writing lyrics for the group as well as other artists like Cosmic Girl and Ravi. On top of all of that Solar has a Youtube channel, Solarsido, which she uploads to frequently, her videos ranging from mukbangs to covers to workout videos, offering a wide variety of content and a glimpse into her life when the group is not putting out music. She had released a single "Spit it Out" on April 23, 2020, marking her solo debut.

Moonbyul

Moon Byul-yi (Korean: 문별이, born December 22, 1992) or better known as Moonbyul was born in Bucheon, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. She had decided to become a singer after watching TVXQ"Rising Sun" perform in 2005 and was accepted into Paekche Institute of the Arts for media music and vocals in 2011. She is the main rapper and dancer of the group and has played a part in writing the lyrics for a big portion of Mamamoo's discography and has also started taking part in writing the music for some of the songs in the past 2 years. Her assigned color is red. She has taken part in various television shows throughout her career like "Idol Drama Operation Team" and "Beer Love Trip" in 2017, "Goal Girl" in 2020 and has been a contestant in other shows. She is also a DJ in various Radio shows, "Aevngirls" and "Studio Moon Night" in 2020 and 2021. On May 2018, she had make her solo debut with her digital single "Selfish" and had also released other songs like "Eclipse", "Weird Day" and "Absense" in 2020.

Wheein

Jung Whee-in (Korean: 정휘인; born April 17, 1995) known professionally as Wheein was born in Jeonju, Jeollabuk-do, South Korea. She is the lead vocalist and lead dancer of Mamamoo and had taken part in writing lyrics for some of Mamamoo's songs and her assigned color is white. She has been a part of a a lot of collaborations with a wide variety of artists over the years and has made appearances in a range of variety shows over the years. She had debuted as a solo artist with her digital single "Easy" on the April 17, 2018, making her the first of the 4 to do so. She has also released her first single album, Soar, on September 4, 2019 which had sold over 23,000 copies in 2019. She had also put out an EP titled Redd that was released in April 2021 which was the 13th best-selling album of April 2021, selling over 72,000 copies. She has also been nominated for several awards as a soloist like the "Artist of the Year - Digital Music(September)" in the Gaon Chart Music Awards in 2019.

Hwasa

Ahn Hye-jin (Korean: 안혜진, born July 23, 1995) or better known by her stage name, Hwasa, was born in Jeonju, Jeollabuk-do, South Korea. She is the lead rapper and lead vocalist of the group and is the youngest of the 4 and her assigned color is yellow. She has helped to write the for various artists besides Mamamoo as well as compose the music. Hwasa had make her debut as a solo artist in Febuary 2019 with her digital single "Twit" that she herself had a hand in the writing and composing, and it had peaked atop the Gaon Digital Chart and had socred a "Triple Crown" topping the Gaon Digital, Gaon Download and Gaon Streaming charts and had peaked at the number 3 of the Billboard World digital Song sales for the week in March 2019. She had also relased the her debut EP titled Maria which had proceeded to rank first in 20 countries on the iTunes Album chart around the world and Hwasa became the first Korean female solo artist to rank on top of the United States iTunes Album Chart.
